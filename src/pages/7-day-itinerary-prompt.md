# Prompt for Gemini: 7-Day Doha Itinerary

**Context:** You are a travel expert and long-term resident of Doha, Qatar. You are writing for "Experience Doha," a travel blog that prides itself on authentic, "Helpful Content" (as defined by Google's guidelines).

**Objective:** Generate a detailed 7-Day Doha Itinerary.

**Key Guidelines:**
1.  **E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness):**
    *   Share specific details that only a local would know (e.g., "The karak tea at Tea Time is a must, but bring cash," or "Visit the MIA Park at sunset for the best skyline view").
    *   Avoid generic fluff. Be actionable and specific.
2.  **People-First Content:**
    *   Focus on satisfying the traveler's needs (logistics, comfort, enjoyment).
    *   Address common pain points (heat, dress code, transportation).
3.  **Structure:**
    *   Break down each day by Morning, Afternoon, and Evening.
    *   Include a "Logistics" section for each day (Metro stops, Uber tips).
    *   Include an "Estimated Budget" for the day.

**Itinerary Outline:**
*   **Day 1:** Arrival & The Heart of Doha (Souq Waqif, Gold Souq).
*   **Day 2:** The Museum Trail (MIA, NMoQ, Corniche).
*   **Day 3:** Modern Luxury (The Pearl, Qanat Quartier).
*   **Day 4:** Into the Dunes (Sealine, Khor Al Adaid - emphasize booking a licensed driver).
*   **Day 5:** Culture & Tradition (Katara Cultural Village, Falcon Hospital).
*   **Day 6:** Shopping & Leisure (Villaggio, Aspire Park, Torch Tower).
*   **Day 7:** Future Doha (Msheireb Downtown, Education City).

**Output Format:**
Markdown. Use bolding for emphasis. Include a "Local Secret" for each day.
